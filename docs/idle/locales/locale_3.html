Once the bustling heart of a thriving trade route, Siltgrave Market now lies half-swallowed by the desert's relentless tides. Time seems arrested here — stalls still stand beneath sun-bleached canopies, and the scent of rare spices lingers faintly in the dry air. Trinkets and baubles remain untouched on weathered carts, as though waiting for their final sale. The whispers of long-dead merchants drift on the wind, some beckoning with deals too good to be true, others locked in ghostly barters that never end. Mirages ripple across the sand-choked avenues, sometimes revealing flashes of the market in its golden prime. Travelers who linger too long risk losing track of reality — or worse, bartering pieces of themselves in otherworldly trades. Strange disturbances in the sand betray unseen movement below. The entire market feels watched, as if the very air expects a transaction... and exacts a price.

Inhabitants

Sand Shades

Wraithlike remnants of traders and thieves who died clutching their goods. They flicker between visibility, attacking from blind angles or slipping into illusions of the living.

Burrowing Scarabs

Chitinous swarms that tunnel beneath the sand, bursting out to overwhelm intruders. Their shimmering shells resemble lost coins, luring the greedy.

Mirage Djinns

Cunning spirits bound to forgotten contracts. They offer travelers powerful boons... but their favors always carry a hidden cost.

Dune Vipers

Long, whip-thin serpents that blend perfectly into the sand. Their venom causes hallucinations of prosperity or doom.

Lost traders

Semi-sentient echoes of past merchants, trapped in endless routines. Some may trade, others beg for escape, and a few lash out in madness.

Glass Beetle

Translucent insectoids whose carapaces refract light into hypnotic patterns. Often found crawling across abandoned relics or mimicking shiny objects.
